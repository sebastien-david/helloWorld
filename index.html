About kingdom sports events

Colchis is the home of king Aeetes, queen Idyia and princess Medea, the Golden Fleece and fire-breathing bulls Khalkotauroi. Colchis is a fabulously wealthy land situated on the mysterious periphery of the heroic world, in the sacred grove of the war god Ares. Colchis is also the land where the mythological Prometheus was punished by being chained to a mountain while an eagle ate at his liver for revealing to humanity the secret of fire. In honor of Zeus, Colchis organizes a series of athletic competitions among representatives of city-states, that are recognized throughout the ancient greece.
